# Rates and Plumbing Desk Guide

**Academy level:** Intermediate  
**Codes:** `RATES`, `PLUMB`, `SOFR`, `MMKT`, `BASIS`, `CURVE`, `AUCT`, `FX`, `NYFED`  
**What you will learn:** How to run the plumbing barometer; which sections map to Academy macro chapters; cross-asset use.

---

## 1. Start at the barometer

Every Rates session:

1. SOFR, EFFR, IORB (or displayed administered rates).  
2. **SOFR − EFFR** spread.  
3. RRP balances / reserves if shown.  
4. Regime label: excess cash vs excess collateral vs balanced.  
5. One sentence journal entry.
6. **Stress strip:** FRED risk pack (VIXCLS, credit, BEI) **plus** primary OFR repo (BGCR/TGCR) and ECB DFR when shown — keyless, not Trading Economics API.

## 3. Cash–futures monitor literacy

Educational monitor ≠ full CTD production system. Use it to feel:

- Futures vs cash yield context  
- SOFR as financing backdrop  
- Why levered basis cares about your barometer

---

## 4. When rates desk rewrites equity study

Open equity vol/GEX immediately if:

- SOFR spikes  
- Basis language stresses  
- Auction tails poorly  
- FX funding looks disordered  

Transmission is the lesson.

---

## 5. Key takeaways

1. Barometer first, narratives second.  
2. Sections are a syllabus in UI form.  
3. Basis monitor teaches funding sensitivity.  
4. FX is funding, not tourism.  
5. Cross-read equities on stress days.
